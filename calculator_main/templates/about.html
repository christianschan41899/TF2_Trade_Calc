What is the TF2 Advanced Trade Calculator?

This trade calculator is made with the purpose to assist traders in dealing with items with add-ons, like Paints or Strange parts. These kinds of items are notoriously difficult to sell for several reasons:

  1. They are a niche item (Only appeal to those who like the paint/part)
  2. Selling the individual items earns more.
  3. Pricing for these items varies heavily and can be difficult to process.

While this site can't help with the first two, I can help by at least alieviating the tedious task of pricing. While I don't expect trading to be radically altered by a site like this, I do expect at least the barrier to entry into this niche market to be lowered by a bit.
